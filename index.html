About Me

My passion for web development started as a hobby when I spent Saturdays developing games and programs for fun while working in real estate and education. I took that passion and decided to make a career out of it by attending the esteemed Coding Bootcamp at the University of Texas, Austin. There I learned to code in several languages and currently specialize in MongoDB, Express, React, and Node (MERN). With my love of puzzles and problem solving, past experience in software programming, and newly established structured and professional instruction in web development, I believe I have the skill set to enhance any web users experience.


I enjoy long walks on the beach, food (both fancy and junk), movies, darts, dark beers, games, and karaoke.
